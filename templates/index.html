About Us

"Mom's Recipe" is not a just phrase used by every Indian to describe how good Mom cooks, it's an emotion that every Indian express towards Mom. Our menu offers something for everybody, to get taste of Mom's food. We offer "Maa ke hath ka khana" for everybody especially for those who are far from home and want to remeber the tase of Mom's Recipe. We at Mom's Recipe have made it our goal to serve truly authentic "Maa ke hath ka khana".
